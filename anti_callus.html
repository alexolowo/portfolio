This Page contains the SolidWorks CAD files and Presentation for an Anti-Callus formation barbell.

This was a project that I conducted research on and designed for my engineering communications course. The aim of the project was to develop a low cost conceptual design for the average gym-goer that has a mechanism to resist callus formation implemented.
