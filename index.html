Engineering Portfolio
I'm a third-year Mechanical (Mechatronics) Engineering student at the University of Toronto who's all about enthusiasm, flexibility, and self-motivation. I love learning and soaking up knowledge to constantly expand my skill set. Currently, I hold the role of Finance Lead for the National Society of Black Engineers UofT Chapter, where I am dedicated to fostering community development and equitable provisions.

This website gives insight into some of the projects that I have assembled/been involved in during my undergraduate studies from classroom work to design team work.
