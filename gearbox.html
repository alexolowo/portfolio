This Page contains the SolidWorks CAD files and Justification for a 12:1 Speed Reduction Gearbox.

This was a project that I designed for my engineering deisgn course. The aim of the project was to develop a low cost conceptual design for a 12:1 Speed Reduction Gearbox. Additionally, the gearbox was to be 3D printed and had to be modified in order to maintain a printing time of under 6 hours.
